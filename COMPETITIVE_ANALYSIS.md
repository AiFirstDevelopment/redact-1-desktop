## Key Insights

1. **Workflow tools** (NextRequest, GovQA, JustFOIA) don't do video redaction
2. **Redaction tools** (Axon, CaseGuard) don't do workflow management
3. **No one** combines both well for small police departments
4. **JustFOIA** is the closest competitor for our target market (law enforcement)
5. **Deadline tracking** is table stakes - every workflow tool has it
